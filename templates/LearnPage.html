PhiPlants Software

Phi Software is a powerful toolkit designed to support researchers in plant metabolomics and LC–MS. It delivers fast, reliable, and efficient solutions—streamlining everything from reading raw data files acquired from instruments to generating comprehensive reports about plant molecules.

Raw File Conversion

Phi leverages the ProteoWizard msconvert package to convert any mass spectrometry raw data folder (from any vendor, e.g., Bruker, Agilent, SCIEX, etc.) into the standard mzML format. During this step, vendor peak-picking algorithms, denoising, centroiding, and other filters are applied

XCMS/CAMERA/MSnbase

Take your analysis further with powerful bioinformatics tools. In this step, Phi applies XCMS algorithms to the mzML files converted earlier, providing robust peak detection and alignment. With CAMERA (Collection of Algorithms for Metabolite Profile Annotation) and MSnbase, you can enrich your workflow with advanced annotation, grouping, and MS data processing—streamlining complex metabolomics analyses with precision and flexibility

Streamline your analysis with flexible annotation options. Use the PlantDB_ESI dataset or your own analyte list to annotate features. When multiple candidates are possible, you can choose the best match with a single click. Focus on the features that matter most, then generate a comprehensive report. Phi automatically saves EICs, MS1, and MS2 spectra (when available) as PNG files, along with a final report sheet summarizing your selected features—all neatly organized in your working directory.

Publications

As every step in this software is scientifically backed and referenced, the data from metabolomics experiments could be published as a paper.
